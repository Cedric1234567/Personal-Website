Hi, I’m Cedric, a third-year Computer Science student at the University of Toronto-Mississauga with a strong passion for software development, particularly in backend and full-stack development, and some interest in data analysis. Over the years, I’ve developed a keen interest in building robust web solutions that address real-world problems. My goal is to create impactful software that serves a greater purpose in fields which help people(for example, health care, sports, banking, etc). I strive to produce solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also make a positive difference for users. I thrive on challenges and am excited by the rapid evolution of the tech industry, which constantly pushes me to learn and grow. Through my academic journey, I’ve acquired critical technical skills, including API integration, efficient code development, and database management. Collaborative coursework has allowed me to complete projects that focus on building APIs, implementing secure and scalable backend architectures, and optimizing algorithms for performance. Beyond academics, I am currently working with peers, specializing in developing web solutions for university clubs such as the Black in STEM Association and the African Student Association. These experiences have not only sharpened my technical skills but paired with my various jobs also enhanced my soft skills, such as teamwork, communication, and leadership—key attributes for thriving in collaborative environments. Outside of academics, I actively participate in networking events, value spending time with my family, keep myself updated on the latest tech, and have a lifelong passion for soccer. Having played the sport for 17 years, including at the semi-professional level in Canada, I’ve had the privilege of traveling internationally, connecting with diverse cultures, and gaining unique perspectives that influence my approach to teamwork and problem-solving. I am eager to further develop my skills through academic, industry, and corporate learning opportunities. I aim to become a software developer capable of producing innovative solutions that positively impact large audiences. Feel free to explore my portfolio or connect with me to discuss opportunities or collaborations.
